Welcome to my personal page

I was born and brought up in Bangladesh. My schooling started with Government Laboratory High School where I studied from 1st grade to 10th grade. Then I went to Dhaka College and finished my 11th and 12th grade education. I did my undergrad and Master of Science from the department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. I also worked as an instructor in different universities in Bangladesh. I first came to the United States in 2013 and started my PhD in Civil & Environmental Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno. While in Reno, I audited some math and physics courses like diffrential geometry, mathematical physics and functional analysis and I really got intereseted in more abstract branches of mathematics. Solving mathematical problems was becoming a daily routine for me. Then I decided to switch to mathematics and moved to Dallas. I started my degree for PhD in Mathematics at the department of Mathematical Sciences in the University of Texas at Dallas in Fall 2015 and finished my PhD concentrating in pure mathematics in Summer 2020. While doing my PhD I also obtained a Master of Science in Mathematics specializing in Data Science. Moreover, I also completed a Graduate Certificate in Data Science. For my PhD research I worked in the field of knot theory under the supervision of Dr. Anh T. Tran.

Story of My Sweetest Achievement

Bangladesh has the system of conducting a combined final exam for 10th grade students which we call Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exam. All the students from the entire country participate in SSC exam. Students can only proceed to 11th grade if the pass SSC exam. The same type of combined final exam is conducted for 12th grade students which we call Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) exam. Without passing HSC studnets are not allowed to start their undergraduate studies. When I appeared for SSC there were 786,220 total candidates and when I appeared for HSC there were total 501,507 students participating. I ranked 1 out in the entire country both in SSC and HSC. Even though at this stage of education these achievements does not matter but I love to remember those memories for a reason. With age we require bigger achievements to be happy but for a kid any achievement is a great source of joy. I never felt such joy of achievements again even though I achived bigger and harder things. That is why this was the sweetest achievement of my life.

My Approach to Life

First of all I know that life is not going to be there forever. In the evolutionary scale human civilization is here on this mother earth for a very short period of time. But no matter how short our existence is, rather than suffering from the the pain of existential crisis we still desire to live better and we get immense joy of discovery through the super power of thinking. We improved ourselves to a point where we can exceed the limitation of our eyes and see through the analysis of our brain. As an example, we can see how the galaxies are or even the whole universe or beyond without going outside of our tiny little planet or in its extreme form without leaving our study room using only a pen and paper we see all possible universes. But this super power of seeing beyond is not a must for survival and we may still fail to survive through a big natural disaster in future. So, there is no point of being afraid to be failure. Rather trying to explore what is beyond my ability is fun. Moreover, this forces the brain to think more efficiently and deeply. That is the reason I do not get afraid to handle challenging works and I love my work. Very rarely I do ad hoc completion of any work. As I am not immortal, I always try to make sure that any work I do must create some permanent windows in the remaining walls surrounding my thinking. I do not feel hesitant to say that my ultimate goal is to achieve the ability of thinking continuously without being tired of going deeper in any angle like an olympic winner gymnast who has the ultimate flexibility of the body, like the voice of a great singer who can travel through all the musical notes with great accuracy or like a fighter jet that has extreme maneuverability and power. I call these freedom. I enjoy my work and fly through my thinking and gradually increasing the maneuverability and strength and reaching towards freedom. I can neither afford to dislike my work nor I can do it ad hoc as the life is short. This is the reason I love my work and I love each moment of my life.
