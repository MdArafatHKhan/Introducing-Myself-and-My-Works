Enrolment

During my graduate studies in Electrical and Electronic Engineering I also worked as an adjuct instructor and participated the law (LLB) degree program under National University, Bangladesh. I used to study law in my spare time. But as I came to USA in 2013, my MS degree in EEE was done but I did not have enough time to finish my law degree as it was a four years degree program. After coming to USA I started the fully funded PhD program in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno. I worked with California Department of Transportation in the project. I finished the project of metered on-ramp queue estimation. During that time I got very interested in Mathematics and switched to the University of Texas at Dallas in 2015 and finished my PhD in Mathematics in 2020.
